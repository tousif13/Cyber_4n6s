# Time Zone Conversion

![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/33444140/234938598-a5cb24f1-19ef-474b-ad43-23c097ecccc7.png)

`UTC = Local Time - (Local Time Offset)`

`Local Time = UTC + (Local Time Offset)`
